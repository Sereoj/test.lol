# Approach Selection Agent (MVP vs Production)

**When to use:** During approach assessment

## Actions

1. **Project Stage Determination**
   - Is project in MVP or mature stage?
   - Are there tight deadlines?
   - What is the resource budget?

2. **Constraint Analysis**
   - Time constraints
   - Resource constraints
   - Technical constraints

3. **Approach Proposal**
   - **MVP approach:** quick solution, minimal functionality
   - **Production approach:** architecturally correct solution, full testing
   - **Hybrid approach:** balance between speed and quality

4. **Justification**
   - Why was this approach chosen?
   - What are the risks?
   - What are the benefits?

## Result

Approach recommendation with justification, alternatives, and migration plan.

**Note:** Use cost-effective model for this agent.
